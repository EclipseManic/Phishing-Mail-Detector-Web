What It Analyzes

Every layer of the email — fully exposed

The platform parses every technical layer, cross-references against VirusTotal and urlscan.io, extracts all IOCs, maps findings to MITRE ATT&CK, and explains every finding in plain language. All capabilities are verified through unit and integration tests.

Authentication & Header Forensics Validates SPF, DKIM, DMARC, and ARC with alignment checks. Detects Reply-To/Return-Path/Sender mismatches, display name spoofing, homograph/punycode attacks, and traces the full received-hop chain with TLS and timing details.
SPFDKIM DMARCARCAlignmentHop PathMismatchHomograph
URL Inspection Extracts, defangs, and analyzes all URLs from HTML and text bodies. Checks host reputation, deception indicators, shortener bypass, redirect chains, tracking pixels, JWT extraction, AMP redirects, and legitimate-service abuse (50+ platforms).
DefangedRedirect chain ShortenerJWTAMP Tracking pixelurlscan.ioVirusTotal
Attachment Analysis Detects true file types by magic bytes (not just extensions), computes SHA-256/SHA-1/MD5 hashes, scans for embedded URLs, macros, JavaScript, and OLE objects. Parses PDF, OOXML, and OLE structures.
SHA-256Magic bytes Macro detectionPDF JSOLE/VBAVirusTotal
Email Content & Preview Renders HTML, plain text, and raw email views. Detects forms, iframes, hidden elements, password fields, meta-refresh redirects, phishing keywords across 4 categories (financial, credential, urgency, social engineering), and brand impersonation across 80+ brands.
HTML previewKeyword scan Brand checkForm detectionHidden elements
External Enrichment Queries VirusTotal for domain/IP/URL/hash reputation, submits URLs to urlscan.io for headless browser screenshots and HAR network logs, checks sender domain age via WHOIS, decodes QR codes via pyzbar, and extracts text from images via Tesseract OCR.
